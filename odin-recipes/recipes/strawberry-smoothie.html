Strawberry Smoothie

A smoothie is a beverage made by puréeing ingredients in a blender. A smoothie commonly has a liquid base, such as fruit juice or milk, yogurt or ice cream. Other ingredients may be added, including fruits, vegetables, non-dairy milk, crushed ice, whey powder or nutritional supplements. Strawberry smoothie is a smoothie made of strawberry.


Ingredients

  • milk
  • yogurt
  • banana
  • strawberries

Step

  1. Add the Liquids.
  2. Add the Yogurt, If Using.
  3. Add the Fruit.
  4. Add the Ice.
  5. Blend and Serve!
